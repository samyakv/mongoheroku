fractional ownership

Each property is divided into a finite number of tokens allowing you to own some share of the tokens and hence a fraction of the property. Based on the token share, owners can collect rent in form of stablecoins sent direclty to their wallets without any hassle.

Organisation owned

Real Estate can't be direclty tokenized. So, each property is owned by a unique entity and that organisation is tokenized allowing purchase of the property on the blockchain.

Zero Management

Each property has a management team working on behalf of the token holders. All you have to worry about is buying the tokens. The management team finds tennants, collects rent, manages repairs so you dont have to.

Easy liquidation

Sell whenever you like without the hassle of finding a broker and waiting months to get a match. Place a bid on our marketplace and enjoy the new found liquidation in such an illiquid market.

As economies grow, the supply of money tends to become less restrictive. Hence borrowing becomes easier, allowing buyers to enter the housing market. Inflation can also play a role in increasing a property's value over time.

Development

Urban growth around a property tends to shoot its price at a very high rate. Also as cities expand, land outside the limits becomes increasingly valuable because of the potential for it to be purchased by developers.

Location

Location is often the biggest factor in appreciation of a property. As the neighbourhood evolves, adding transit routes, schools, shopping centers and more, it causes the properties value to climb.

improvements

Home improvements also tend to affect appreciation. Adding an extra room, remodeling the kitchen, updating the flooring are just some ways a property owner may try to increase the value of the property.
